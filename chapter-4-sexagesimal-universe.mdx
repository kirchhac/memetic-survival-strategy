Specifically:

- The 4-minute drift (sidereal vs solar)

Together these produced the sexagesimal universe.

## III. The Four-Minute Drift Creates the 60-Minute Hour

From Chapter 2:

Earth rotates 1° in 4 minutes when measured by the stars.

Therefore 15° = 1 hour (because 15 × 4 minutes = 60 minutes).

Therefore 24 hours × 15° = 360°.

Thus:

⭐ **The 60-minute hour is a direct side-effect of Earth's orbital motion.**

Not a human invention.

Not a Babylonian whim.

It is the only number that fits the sky's structure.

The hour exists because:

- 1° takes 4 minutes
- 15° takes 60 minutes
- 360° completes the cycle

**The heavens demanded a sexagesimal system.**

## V. The Sun and Moon Require Sexagesimal Arithmetic

- The solar year ≈ 365 days

- Difference ≈ 11 days

Intercalation is messy.

Only base-60 can:

- harmonize 11- and 30-day corrections
- divide the year into 12 months
- reconcile the Moon's 29.53-day synodic cycle
- subdivide angles for star risings
- measure unequal seasonal lengths
- handle precessional cycles
- map the Sun's daily 1° drift
- convert 360° into 36 decans and 72 half-decans
- subdivide zodiac signs into 30° and 3° segments

**Sexagesimal math wasn't chosen—it was forced by the sky.**

The lunar-solar misalignment can only be tracked accurately on a base-60 system.
